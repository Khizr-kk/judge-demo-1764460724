## Idea Summary
A SaaS platform utilizing AI to automate content generation for small businesses, focusing on social media posts and blog articles.

## Problem Statement
Small businesses struggle with consistent, high-quality content creation due to limited time, budget, and expertise, leading to poor online presence and engagement.

## Target Persona
Local small business owner

## MVP Features
- AI-powered social media post generation (multiple platforms)
- AI-powered blog article generation (short-form)
- Customizable content parameters (tone, length, keywords, topic)
- Industry-specific content templates
- Content editing and refinement tools
- Content saving and organization
- User dashboard for content management

## Tech Stack
Modern web framework (e.g., React/Next.js for frontend, Python/FastAPI or Node.js/Express for backend), PostgreSQL for database, cloud-based infrastructure (AWS/GCP/Azure), and integration with fine-tuned open-source LLMs or commercial AI APIs for content generation.
